Let us introduce ourselves



We understand that learning something takes time and effort while life can sometimes get in the way. Here at First Launch Tutoring, we have the knowledge and resources to help you find the perfect tutor for your needs. We provide you with one-on-one tutoring based on your specific learning needs. Our tutors are qualified and passionate about helping you succeed on your learning journey. Our tutors work with you to understand how you learn best and help you achieve the results you are looking for. We are here to help you make the learning process a little easier and more fun, whether it's getting assistance with a tricky subject, learning the piano or taking on a new language.




Our services



Online Tutoring

We offer online lessons! Your can meet your tutor online using an online conferencing platform. Our tutors are experts in their subjects from primary school to university and are trained to fully utilise the online platform. The online classroom features everything you need for an interactive learning experience. Make notes on the whiteboard, share your screen and use the chat function - it's the perfect tool for online learning.



In-Person Tutoring

Meet with your tutor in person! You can find our experience tutors across South Africa. Meet up with your tutor at your home, their home or even at a local spot. Our handpicked tutors strive to help you succeed on your learning journey while ensuring all safety precautions are taken.
